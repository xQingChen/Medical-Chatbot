Melting Cherry Tomato & Mozzarella Pasta (30 mins)

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces whole-wheat rotini or other short tube pasta
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼-½ teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1 pound cherry tomatoes (about 3 cups)
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ¼ teaspoon ground pepper
  • ⅓ cup chopped fresh basil, plus more for garnish
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic glaze, plus more for drizzling
  • 1 cup halved mozzarella pearls

Crispy Salmon Rice Bowl (25 mins)

Ingredients

  • 1 pound skinless center-cut salmon fillet, cut into 1 1/2-inch chunks
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • ¼ cup teriyaki glaze, preferably low-sodium
  • 2 cups hot cooked brown rice
  • 1 cup cooked shelled edamame
  • 2 scallions, sliced
  • 1 cup diced cucumber
  • 1 medium avocado, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons Sriracha mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame seeds (optional)
